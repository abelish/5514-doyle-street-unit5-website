About Doyle Street Cohousing

Doyle Street Cohousing functions as a village to foster connection. Our diverse community consists of twelve homes where residents participate in the life of the community through cooking, dining, maintaining the facility, and playing together.

Community Dinners

Optional dinners twice a week in the common house, with rotating cooking responsibilities (approximately every 6-8 weeks)

Shared Responsibilities

4 hours/month community maintenance, 1.5-hour monthly HOA meetings, and 12 hours/year workdays

Community Governance

Every adult resident is a full voting member of the Doyle Street Cohousing Association (DSCA)

About Us

The ten years we spent living in cohousing are filled with happy memories. From spontaneous game nights, to community dance parties, movies in the common house, water balloon fights in the parking lot, and on one particularly memorable occasion a pig roast complete with fire spinning, we cherish the connection and community that is unique to cohousing.

This community is important to us. It is our goal to make the sale process as human as possible while still adhering to standard protocols and protections for all parties involved. We are looking for a buyer who shares the values intrinsic to cohousing and genuinely desires to live in community.
